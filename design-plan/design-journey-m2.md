## Interactivity Rationale (Milestone 2)
> Describe the purpose of your proposed interactivity.<br>
> Provide a brief rationale explaining how your proposed interactivity addresses the goals of your site's audience and enhances their user experience.

Our website has a bunch of Ivy's artworks under different sections. If we stack all these images vertically for narrow design, this will occupy too much space. Therefore, we use image carousel and image gallery to display multiples images. For carousel, our audience can click the left and right arrows to view images. And for gallery, our audience can simply click the image thumbnails they're curious about to see a larger and clearer version of image as well as corresponding descriptions. Both two interactivity designs help audience be fully immersive in the process of knowing about Ivy's artistic style and trace which image they're looking at. And those designs also fit the artistic theme of our website.
