### Client Questionnaire (Milestone 1)
> Develop a client questionnaire to learn more about your client and their goals.
> You may use the questionnaire below, you may modify the questionnaire below, or you may create your own.

1. Describe your portfolio and its purpose.

    - My portfolio is primarily an art website that will showcase my artwork

2. What makes your portfolio special or unique?

    - My portfolio will consist of my original artwork and personal anecdotes, making it unique.

3. Why are you looking for a new website or updating your existing site?

    - I want to be able to share my artwork quickly and professionally to someone who I might want to work with or work for. Additionally, since I plan to apply to art schools and art programs, many of them require a personal portfolio or portfolio website.

4. Who do you want to visit your site? Who do you believe is your website's intended audience?

    - Young adults (peers in the art community) and admissions offices

5. When visiting your site, what actions do you want your visitors to take?

    - I want them to be able to browse through my different artwork and read about my art process and decisions made during the creation of each piece. I also want my contact info readily available to them in case they want to reach out to me.

6. What are your goals for this site?

    - To have a consolidated space that professionally showcases my talent as an artist. I want this to easily get across my talents to those I send it to. I want someone to be able to look at this website and recognize the work I've put into my art and recognize who I am as an individual and as an artist.

7. Is there a "feel" that you want for your site? Are there colors or imagery that you have in mind?

    - Clean and professional, simple and maybe just neutral colours so it doesn't take away from my artwork (white, beige, black etc)

8. What are your three favorite competitors or similar organizations' websites? Why?

    - https://www.helenjee.com/

    - https://www.devinellekurtz.com/

    - https://emilyxustory.weebly.com/

    - These are artists that I admire because of their vibrant and animated-but-detailed art form. I enjoy the simplicity of their websites, as it helps their artwork stand out.
